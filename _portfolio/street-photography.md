A series of street photography captured in the bustling streets of New York City. This project focuses on the candid moments and unique characters that define urban life.

The photographs were taken over a period of three months, exploring different neighborhoods and capturing the rhythm of the city. Each image tells a story of everyday life in one of the world's most vibrant cities.

**Technical Notes:**
- Shot primarily in black and white
- Natural light only
- Candid approach to maintain authenticity
