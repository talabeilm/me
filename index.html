About Me

Ademola Oladipo, an emerging artist in the world of photography, hails from the vibrant city of Lagos, Nigeria, and presently calls Toronto, Canada, his creative home.

His work is driven by a fascination with emotions, influenced by cinema and paintings. To explore this, He photographs the human body in sparse, open settings, using these spaces as a metaphor for our inner landscape.
